2. What are cookies and similar technologies?

  • Cookies are small text files stored on your device by your browser.
  • SDKs / local storage / session storage store data in your app or browser similar to cookies.
  • Pixels / tags / scripts load from our site or third parties to enable analytics, ads, and security.

We refer to all of the above as "cookies".

3. Legal bases & scope

  • In the UK/EEA/Switzerland, we only set non-essential cookies (e.g., analytics, advertising, personalisation) with your consent (UK PECR / EU ePrivacy). Strictly necessary cookies do not require consent.
  • In certain regions (e.g., US states with opt-out laws), we respect applicable opt-out signals and provide "Do Not Sell/Share" style controls where relevant.
  • We honour Global Privacy Control (GPC) where feasible.

4. Types of cookies we use

A. Strictly Necessary (Essential)

Purpose: security, load balancing, user login, subscription access (PRO), shopping/checkout flows for advertisers, consent storage, and fraud prevention.

Consent: Not required.

Examples: session ID, CSRF token, consent record, payment verification.

B. Preferences (Functionality)

Purpose: remember settings (language, region/currency, accessibility), saved searches.

Consent: Required in UK/EEA/CH.

C. Performance (Analytics & Measurement)

Purpose: measure usage, diagnose issues, improve features (e.g., Google Analytics with IP masking).

Consent: Required in UK/EEA/CH.

D. Advertising & Marketing

Purpose: deliver, cap frequency, personalise, and measure ads; attribute conversions; detect invalid traffic.

Consent: Required in UK/EEA/CH. We label paid placements as "Sponsored".

E. Social media / Embedded content (if used)

Purpose: share buttons, embeds (e.g., YouTube), which may set their own cookies.

Consent: Required in UK/EEA/CH.

6. Consent management (banner & preference center)

On your first visit (and periodically thereafter), we display a cookie banner allowing you to:

  • Accept all, Reject non-essential, or Manage settings by category/purpose;
  • Read this Cookie Policy;
  • Reopen the preference center anytime via the "Manage Cookies" footer link;
  • Withdraw or change consent at any time (changes apply going forward).

We maintain a consent record (timestamp, purposes, region, version of partners/purposes). If the set of partners/purposes changes materially, we will ask you to review your choices again.

Banner copy (example, short):

We use cookies to operate the site, improve performance, and (with your consent) personalise ads. Click Accept all to consent, Reject non-essential, or Manage settings to choose. See our Cookie Policy for details.

7. How to control cookies

  • Preference Center: Use Manage Cookies (footer) to change settings anytime.
  • Browser settings: Most browsers let you block or delete cookies. Blocking essential cookies may break site functionality.
  • Ads settings: For Google ads personalisation, visit your Google Ads Settings; broader industry opt-outs (where available) are provided in the preference center.
  • Global Privacy Control (GPC): If your browser sends a GPC signal, we treat it as an opt-out for applicable regions/purposes.

8. Retention

  • Session cookies expire when you close your browser/app.
  • Persistent cookies generally last 1–24 months unless you clear them earlier; some ad-measurement cookies may refresh upon consent.
  • Payment/anti-fraud cookies can persist as required to prevent abuse and comply with legal obligations.
  • We routinely review lifetimes to apply the shortest practical durations.
